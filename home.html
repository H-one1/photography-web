Photography

Photography is the art, science, and practice of creating durable images by recording light, either electronically using an image sensor or chemically using a light-sensitive material such as photographic film.

Videography

Videography is the art and practice of capturing moving images on electronic media, such as videotape, digital media, or streaming platforms.

Photo Retouching

Photo retouching is the process of altering and enhancing images to improve their appearance, correct imperfections, and achieve a desired aesthetic.

Aerial Photography

Aerial photography is a type of photography that involves capturing images from an elevated position, typically using aircraft, drones, balloons, or other aerial platforms.

Event photography

Event photography is a specialized branch of photography focused on capturing moments and experiences during various types of event.
